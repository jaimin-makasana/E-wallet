The world is leading to technology era very rapidly. Very soon paper money will no longer be king in world of payments. Our project “Cashless Travel” will give travelling a modern prospect. Basic functionality of our project is to provide a journey without any ‘cash, changes and tickets’. The technology used in project is quite simple that any ordinary, non-technical person can use it very easily yet so efficient and potent. The ultimate aim of project is to deliver the most secure and finest travel experience without a single coin of currency.
